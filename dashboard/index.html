💡 Key Business Insights

1

West Region Dominates

The West region contributes ~32% of total sales, driven by California and Washington markets.

2

Office Supplies = Profit Engine

Despite lower revenue, Office Supplies achieves the highest profit margins (30–45%).

3

Q4 Holiday Spike

Sales consistently spike 40%+ during Oct–Dec across all years due to holiday shopping.

4

Heavy Discounts Hurt Profits

Discounts ≥25% cause ~40% of Furniture orders to lose money. Cap at 20% recommended.

5

Corporate Has Higher AOV

Consumer drives 52% of volume, but Corporate segment has 25–30% higher Average Order Value.
